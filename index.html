What we do

  • We are working with tech startups in Ghana to provide seed funding and support systems to scale up projects and grow their companies to become the next ‘unicorns’ coming out of Africa.
  • We are providing a network of experienced tech entrepreneurs both from Africa, Europe and the USA to share and mentor startups incubated with us on how to run businesses and develop great products that customers will pay for.
  • We are providing working space and a technical team to guide entrepreneurs to take products to market. The whole process of go-to-market shall be guided by an excellent team of experts from various key departments – Marketing, Sales and Distribution, Customer Management, Finance and Legal.
Who are we looking for?
  • Partners - To work with us in both executive and non-executive roles to set up a fully functional business incubator.
  • Investors - To back the incubator’s VC fund that will invest seed funds in the incubated portfolio companies to the tune of $25,000 per startup in convertible debt.
  • Mentors – Seasoned tech entrepreneurs to provide direct advice and mentorship to the startup companies on how to develop great products.
  • Experts – Experienced persons in Marketing, Sales and Distribution, Customer Management, Finance and Legal to work with the start-up companies on a daily basis to develop and take products to market.
